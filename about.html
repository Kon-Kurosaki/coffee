About us

A cozy corner for coffee lovers and conversation seekers

At Phana Coffee, every morning begins with thoughtful roasts, warm hospitality, and a place to slow down. We source beans directly from growers, roast in small batches, and serve each cup with the same care we would pour for friends.

Our shop is built for lingering — quiet corners for work, bright tables for catch-ups, and a menu designed to pair with whatever rhythm your day takes.
